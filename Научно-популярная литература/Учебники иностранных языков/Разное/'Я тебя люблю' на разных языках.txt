Explanation of Accents
----------------------
a'		-&gt; 'a' with the acute accent (') over it, accent aigu
		   (ASCII code 160)
a"		-&gt; 'a' with two dots (Umlaut)   (ASCII code 132)
a^		-&gt; elongated vowel (e.g. 2 a's)
a~		-&gt; 'a' with a tilde(~) over it
e^		-&gt; 'e' with a carot(^) over it
e'		-&gt; 'e' with the acute accent (') over it (ASCII code 130)
n~		-&gt; 'n' with a tilde(~) over it
o~		-&gt; 'o' with a tilde(~) over it
